Choosing Plants

Choosing the right plants is one of the most exciting steps for beginners. Start with plants that are easy to care for and grow quickly. Herbs such as basil, mint, and parsley are great because they sprout fast and are useful in cooking. For vegetables, try lettuce, tomatoes, or green beans. These are common beginner-friendly crops. If you want flowers, marigolds and sunflowers are good options because they are hardy and brighten up any garden. When choosing, consider your available space and sunlight. If you only have partial sun, pick shade-tolerant plants like spinach or kale. Container plants should be compact and not require deep roots.
