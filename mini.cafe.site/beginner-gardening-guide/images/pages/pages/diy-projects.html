DIY Projects

Gardening becomes more fun when you add creativity. DIY projects allow you to recycle materials and save money while improving your garden. One simple project is making pots from old containers such as plastic bottles, tin cans, or jars. Just add drainage holes and decorate them. Another idea is creating a compost bin from a plastic bucket. Composting reduces kitchen waste and gives you natural fertilizer. For small spaces, try a vertical garden using hanging shoe organizers or wooden pallets. You can grow herbs or flowers in each pocket.
