Bohol Chocolate Hills

Bohol is famous for its unique geological formations known as the Chocolate Hills. These hills turn brown during the dry season, resembling chocolate mounds, hence the name. The area is also rich in biodiversity and offers a glimpse of the Tarsier, one of the world's smallest primates.
